## What Nudge Does
Nudge is an AI business operating system that connects your inbox, tasks, calendar, and team in one place.

- **Email Logs** — AI automatically categorizes every email into Meeting Request, Task Needed, Needs Reply, or FYI
- **Work Board** — Kanban board with AI bottleneck detection, task prediction, and live board updates
- **Schedule** — AI builds your optimal day from emails and calendar. Detects scheduling conflicts automatically
- **Inner** — Real-time team collaboration with task locking and live chat
- **Business OS** — Owner dashboard with departments, agents, routing rules, and company email management
- **Nudge AI** — Floating AI assistant that reads your emails, creates meetings, drafts replies, and navigates the workspace

## Business Impact
- Saves 4.3 hours per day on email management
- Automatically routes support emails to the right team
- Detects overdue invoices and financial events from inbox
- Creates Google Meet links and sends invitations in one command
- Real-time team collaboration reduces response time by 20%

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend** — HTML, CSS, Vanilla JS deployed on Cloudflare Pages
- **Database** — Supabase (PostgreSQL) with real-time subscriptions
- **AI** — Groq API (Llama 3.3 70B) for fast intelligent responses
- **Auth** — Supabase Auth with Google OAuth
- **Deployment** — Cloudflare Pages
